Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Clock ++ uses cookies to help Clock ++ identify and track visitors, their usage of Clock ++ website, and their website access preferences. Clock ++ visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Clock ++’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Clock ++’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
